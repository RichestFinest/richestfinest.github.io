About Riche$t Fine$t

Richest Finest is a daily comic strip written and illustrated by Kritin "Rusticism" Neela about a rich (but awful) boy named Ryan and his best friend Brad, who tackle (self-inflicted) issues in an ever-changing world of adventure, drama, and money.

About the Author

Kritin Neela, also known as Rusticism, is the writer, illustrator, and creator of twin comic series Richest Finest and John Voyage. He currently resides in Ottawa, Canada, where he makes comics (almost) daily and publishes them to, well, here. In his free time, he enjoys gaming.
